# Tyled

Take control of the board in this fast-paced, top-down strategy duel! Spread your color by firing powerful beams that target a single tile per shot—either the tile at the edge of the board or the one just before the first colored tile in their path. Step on enemy tiles to take damage, or unleash your beam to strike opponents directly, carrying them along its path and dealing massive damage. Parry incoming beams to reverse them, triggering high-speed counterchains that can swing the match in an instant. Master timing, positioning, and resource management as you race to dominate lanes, claim territory, and outwit your opponent in a deadly tactical showdown.
